## Full description (max 4000 chars)
```
Reframe is a private, gentle journal for the classic CBT thought record — a simple, well-researched way to work through upsetting moments and find a calmer, more balanced perspective.

When a thought is weighing on you, Reframe walks you through it one step at a time:

1. Situation — what actually happened
2. Moods — what you felt, and how strongly
3. Automatic thoughts — and the "hot thought" driving the feeling
4. Evidence for it
5. Evidence against it
6. A more balanced, realistic thought
7. How you feel now

Over time, this practice helps train a fairer, kinder inner voice.

WHY PEOPLE CHOOSE REFRAME

• Truly private — everything stays on your device. No account, no sign-up, no cloud, no tracking, no ads.
• Works fully offline — open it anywhere, anytime.
• Capture-first — jot a thought in seconds and finish the reframe later. Drafts are saved automatically, so you never lose your place.
• Gentle and clear — plain language, with a 1-minute primer for anyone new to CBT.
• Insights — see your mood before vs. after, your progress over time, and the thinking traps you notice most.
• Daily reminder — an optional, gentle nudge to check in.
• Lock it down — protect your entries with a PIN or fingerprint/face unlock.
• Export anytime — share or back up your records as PDF, JSON, or CSV. Import them back whenever you like.
• Support when you need it — quick access to crisis lines and a grounding exercise.

GROUNDED IN RESEARCH

CBT and the thought record are among the most studied tools in mental health. Reframe is built around the standard 7-column approach used in clinical practice. (Reframe is a self-help tool, not therapy.)

IMPORTANT

Reframe is for self-help and general wellbeing. It is not a medical device and not a substitute for professional care or diagnosis. If you are struggling or in crisis, please reach out to a qualified professional or a crisis line — there are links inside the app.

Start with one thought. See how it feels to look at it clearly.
```
