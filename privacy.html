Privacy Policy — Reframe

Last updated: 7 June 2026

The short version: Reframe keeps everything on your device. We don’t have an account system, we don’t run analytics or ads, and your thought records never leave your phone unless you explicitly export or share them yourself.

1. Who we are

Reframe (“the app”, “we”) is a self-help journalling app for the Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) 7-column thought record. For any privacy questions, contact: [your-contact-email@example.com].

2. Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. Reframe has no user accounts, no sign-in, and no servers that receive your data. Specifically:

  • Your thought records, moods, notes, and settings are stored only on your device (in the app’s local database).
  • We do not use advertising, third-party analytics, trackers, cookies, or advertising identifiers.
  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to us or to anyone else.

3. How your data is used and stored

All data you enter stays in local storage on your device so the app can show you your history and insights. It remains until you delete individual records, use Erase all data in Settings, or uninstall the app.

4. Sharing and exporting (only when you choose)

The app lets you export your records as a PDF, JSON, or CSV file, and import a backup. These actions are entirely under your control. When you tap share/export, your device’s own sharing or save dialog handles the file — where it goes is determined by you and the app or service you send it to. We are not involved.

5. Optional device features

  • Reminders: if you enable a daily reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device via the operating system. No reminder data is sent anywhere.
  • App lock: if you set a PIN or enable biometric unlock, it is verified locally on your device. We never receive it.

6. Children

Reframe is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no data, no personal information from children is gathered.

7. Security

Because your information never leaves your device, the main protection is your device itself. We recommend using your device’s screen lock and the app’s optional PIN/biometric lock. Exported files are your responsibility once they leave the app.

8. Not medical advice

Reframe is a self-help tool and is not a medical device, diagnosis, or treatment, and is not a substitute for professional care. If you are in crisis, please use the in-app support resources or contact a local emergency service or crisis line.

9. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the revised version at this URL.
